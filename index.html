FAQ

Everything you need to know about YES ACADEMY's courses, enrollments, and study abroad services.

What courses does YES ACADEMY offer? ▼
YES ACADEMY offers IELTS Academic, IELTS General Training, PTE Academic/UKVI, PTE Core, Basic Grammar to Advance, and PTE Booster courses. We also provide comprehensive Study Abroad Consultancy services including university selection, SOP writing, and visa guidance.
How long does each course take? ▼
Course durations vary by program. IELTS and PTE courses typically run for 4–12 weeks depending on the student's existing level and target score. Grammar courses run for 6–8 weeks. We also offer intensive crash courses for students with upcoming exam dates. Contact us for a personalized study plan.
Do you offer free trial classes or counselling? ▼
Yes! We offer a FREE one-on-one counselling session for all prospective students. During this session, we assess your current English level, understand your target score and goals, and recommend the most suitable course for you. Click "Get Free Counselling" anywhere on this page to book yours.
What is the batch size for classes? ▼
We maintain small batches of 8–12 students to ensure every student receives personalized attention and direct feedback from instructors. This intimate learning environment is one of the key reasons for our 95% success rate.
How do I enroll in a course? ▼
You can enroll by clicking any "Enroll Now" button on this page, calling us directly, messaging us on WhatsApp, or visiting our center in Sylhet. Our team will guide you through a quick enrollment process and help you choose the right batch timing.
Do you provide study abroad consultancy services? ▼
Absolutely! Our study abroad services include university shortlisting, Statement of Purpose (SOP) writing, application processing, scholarship guidance, visa application support, and pre-departure briefings. We have helped students secure admissions in the UK, Canada, Australia, USA, and Europe.
What is your success rate guarantee? ▼
We maintain a consistent 95% success rate in helping students achieve their target scores. While we cannot guarantee specific scores (as results depend on individual effort), we commit to providing the best possible training, materials, and support. Students who complete the full course and follow our practice schedule see dramatic improvements in their band scores.
Can I book a mock test without enrolling in a full course? ▼
Yes! We offer standalone mock test sessions for both IELTS and PTE. These full-length simulated tests are conducted under real exam conditions and include detailed score analysis and feedback. Click "Book Mock Test" to schedule your session.
